# Privacy Policy — YouTube Channel Blocker

Last updated: 2026-06-17

YouTube Channel Blocker does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal
data.

The only data the extension saves is your list of blocked channels and your
filter preferences. This is stored using Chrome's storage API on your own device
and, if you have Chrome Sync enabled, synced across your own signed-in Chrome
browsers by Google. This data is never sent to the developer or any third party.

The extension runs only on youtube.com. It does not track your browsing, collect
analytics, or contact any external server.
